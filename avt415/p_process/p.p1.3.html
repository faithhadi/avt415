User Scenario Mapping

Actor:

Busy Professional Ben

Motivator:

Needs a convenient, healthy caffeine option that doesn’t take too much time, especially during his hectic workdays.

Intention:

Wants to find a reliable and convenient matcha tea brand that gives him energy but doesn’t take up too much time in his busy schedule.

Action:

Ben browses online for a matcha tea brand that offers both ready-to-drink options and matcha powder he can use to make quick drinks at home. He discovers a brand that offers single-serve matcha sachets or matcha in convenient resealable pouches.

Resolution:

Finds the matcha sachets easy to use in his morning routine, giving him the boost he needs without any hassle.
